What is Propylene Glycol USP?

Propylene glycol USP is a clear, colorless, and odorless liquid with a sweet and slightly pungent taste. It is a type of polyol, a broad class of compounds used in various industrial, pharmaceutical, and food applications. The USP designation stands for United States Pharmacopeia, a standards-setting organization that sets guidelines for the quality and purity of pharmaceuticals and other healthcare products.

How does it work?

Propylene glycol USP is a key ingredient in the production of various products, including:

  • Pharmaceuticals: It is used as a solvent, excipient, and carrier in the manufacture of pills, capsules, and tablets.
  • Food and beverages: It is used as a sweetener, emulsifier, and stabilizer in the production of food products, such as ice cream, frozen yogurt, and salad dressings.
  • Cosmetics: It is used as a moisturizer, emollient, and humectant in the production of skincare and haircare products.
  • Industrial applications: It is used as a cleaning agent, degreaser, and lubricant in various industrial processes.
